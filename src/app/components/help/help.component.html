How do I create a new writing plan?

To create a plan, navigate to the "Create A Plan" page from the sidebar. Enter your project name, target word count (or time goal), and select your start and end dates. You can also choose a logging strategy (Steady, Rising, etc.) to customize your daily targets.

What is the difference between specific strategies?

Steady: Distributes your goal evenly across all days.
Rising: Starts with a lower daily goal and increases gradually.
Random: Varies the daily goal to keep things unpredictable.
Whichever: Allows you to write however much you want, recalculating daily.

How do I edit a previous day's log?

Go to your Plan Detail page (click on a plan name). In the Calendar view below the chart, click on any past date. A popup will appear allowing you to edit the word count for that specific day.
